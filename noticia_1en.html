BeClever S.A. introduced its new website

The Leader in Latin America, developer of advanced products for credit businesses, presented this afternoon at the PILP II of the City of La Punta (San Luis), its renewed web platform.

The Leader company in Latin America, developer of advanced products for credit businesses, presented this afternoon at PILP II in the City of La Punta (San Luis), its renewed web platform. It aims at the consultation of existing products, and the presentation of new ones, which are already in an advanced stage of development.

The institutional communication of the firm will be another of the high points of the web. "We needed a renewal in terms of the image of BeClever S.A. Communication technologies are constantly evolving and we considered that adding in this field will accompany the growth of the company experienced in recent years" commented Gustavo Defilpo, director of the entity.
