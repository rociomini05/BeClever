Virtual wallets, essential tools

The concept of “globalization” has never been better represented by the impact generated by the Covid-19 Virus.

The concept of “globalization” has never been better represented by the impact generated by the Covid-19 Virus.

The “pandemic” effect and its consequences in each region, exponentiated the vulnerability in which the nations of the globe live immersed. Health, economy, politics, culture, sports, society ... represent some of the topics that were savagely affected by this virus. However, the reaction capacity of each state was reflected in the choice of the tools used to give some continuity to the actions usually carried out.

The implementation of restrictive policies framed in the motto "stay at home", proclaimed an accelerated evolution of existing commercial forms, which had not yet reached such high popularity peaks as they do today. Initially, the "Virtual Wallets" and "Electronic Payments", emerged as a good commercial practice for the optimization of times, transfers, physical spaces, and other aspects of comfort for both Users and Companies. The need to administer a secure instrument, which was capable of reducing cash circulation, avoiding conglomerates in banks and other collectors, raised the level of confidence about this solution, which, broadly speaking, allowed individuals to pay services, face debts , receive payments or state aid without leaving their homes; especially for those ‘unbanked’.

Currently, virtual wallets consolidate an efficient platform for a village made up of Governments-Shops-Users, who find in it a necessary means for health care and for compliance with pre-established protocols.

“For some time now, we have been developing our own virtual wallet with the idea of ​​complementing our suite of products and services. The pandemic, without a doubt, led us to speed up production times in order to make an efficient tool available to our customers. We understood the urgent need of each entity to be able to provide reliable online services for the continuity of its operations without the loss of competitiveness. Clever Wallet® was conceived and designed to be implemented in any type of business and industry ”, commented Gustavo Defilpo, president of BeClever S.A.
