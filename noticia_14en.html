Construction of Scorecard and Design of Origination Strategies

The seminar "Construction of Scorecard and Design of Origination Strategies" will be held by Lic. Flavio Covacich



On April 12, in the “Meeting” room of the hotel –Recoleta Grand– of the Autonomous City of Buenos Aires, the seminar "Construction of Scorecard and Design of Origination Strategies" will be held at charge of Lic. Flavio Covacich.

Organized by Campus Conferences and under the auspices of BeClever SA, the workshop is aimed at different agents of the financial industry who wish to optimize methodologies in the construction of scoring models and in the application of origination strategies for the granting of credits.

The intense day plans to combine: development of theories, examples through strategies of use of each model and practical exercises for the application and fixing of the acquired knowledge. It should be noted that it is not necessary to have previous statistical instruction since statistical tools and procedures will not be studied in depth.

The preset axes in the schedule are:



• Credit Scoring Model

What can you predict ?. Variables that you use. Scoring application in the different stages of the client's cycle. Objectives and impact of the use of Scoring models.

• Scoring Stages and Methodology

Stages for the development of a Scoring model. Methodologies. Portfolio characteristics: ‘Retail’ - ‘Corporate’. Applications.

• Scoring Development

Definition ‘Good’ / ‘Bad’. Sampling window. Analysis and selection of variables. Inference about rejected. Model building techniques.

• Scorecard and Strategy Design

Result of the model. Origination strategies. Optimal definition of the cut-off point (Cut Off). Rejection and approval levels: growth in clients and portfolio. Cost reduction. Discussion of results.

• Scorecard Use Strategies

Examples of use: Credit limit management. Card 'renewal / expiration' date. Cross selling. Control of delinquency. Overdrafts. Decision to 'Pay' / 'Do Not Pay'. Business conditions (rates, expenses, terms, etc).
